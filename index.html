Huy Dinh

Full-stack web developer, Tokyo, Japan

Hey there.

I’m Huy Dinh, a 32-year-old Vietnamese web developer. I work at VELTRA Corporation where we build and operate large-scale web products. I have a logical mind, a hardwired desire to be better, and a strong ambition to make a difference on the web.

Since I graduated in 2008, I have been working for 10 years on many web projects, big and small. In 2015, I received the PhD title in Information Science and Technology from The University of Tokyo, where I worked as a postdoc for a few months before fully coming back to the industry. That special experience equipped me with some useful skills, such as perceiving complex problems and presenting complex ideas. Thus, I think it would be interesting to share my story, and my take on new and trending things in the tech world.
